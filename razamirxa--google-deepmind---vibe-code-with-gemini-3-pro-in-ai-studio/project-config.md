*Appears in: All 3 files (Gemini, Claude, GPT)*

**Why it's strong:** Addresses the biggest education pain point (homework struggle), uses Gemini 3's unique reasoning capabilities, and has clear emotional impact for demo video.

## 📝 **250-WORD WRITEUP TEMPLATE** (for TimeCapsule)

**Title:** TimeCapsule - History Comes Alive
**Subtitle:** Animate textbook portraits and interview historical figures with AI

**Description (248 words):**

History textbooks are filled with portraits of influential figures, yet students struggle to connect emotionally with people reduced to dates and static images. This disconnect leads to surface-level memorization instead of deep historical understanding.

TimeCapsule transforms any historical figure's photograph into an interactive, animated conversation partner using Gemini 3 Pro's Veo animation, conversational AI, and real-time fact-checking. Students upload a textbook portrait, and within seconds, the figure comes to life—eyes blinking, lips moving, ready to share their story.

Using Gemini's multimodal capabilities, students engage in voice conversations with historically accurate responses. Want to ask Abraham Lincoln about his doubts before issuing the Emancipation Proclamation? Marie Curie about her groundbreaking research? The AI maintains character consistency while drawing on Google Search integration to provide factually verified historical context.

The app uses:
- Veo 3 (`movie`) for portrait animation
- Conversational voice AI (`audio_spark`, `voice_chat`, `speech_to_text`) for natural dialogue
- Google Search (`google`) for real-time fact verification
- Advanced reasoning (`network_intelligence`) for contextual, emotionally nuanced responses

This approach makes history tangible for visual and auditory learners, transforms passive reading into active engagement, and helps students develop empathy for historical figures as complex humans rather than textbook entries.

Impact: 260+ million secondary students worldwide study history. TimeCapsule can democratize engaging historical education globally, improving retention and fostering critical thinking about the human stories behind historical events.
