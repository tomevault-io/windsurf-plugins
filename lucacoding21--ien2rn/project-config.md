### Summary

The team established a simple, clear, and recruitment-focused website design, confirming user flows and the need for a revised Statement of Work.

**Review Client Websites**  
William Nguyen showcased the aluminum railing manufacturer website, the team’s largest client, to demonstrate design versatility. KM emphasized keeping the IN2RN website simple, targeting an older demographic that needs easy navigation.
