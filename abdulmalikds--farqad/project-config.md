This project is a chatbot that helps users manage their personal finances.

Features:
- The chatbot can talk in both Arabic and English.
- Users can ask questions about their spending, saving, and budgeting.
- The chatbot can answer in a friendly and helpful way.
- Users can upload financial documents (like receipts or statements) for analysis.
- The chatbot gives advice on saving money and making better financial choices.
- Users get budget insights and summaries through a simple dashboard.
- The chatbot connects to a backend that uses AI to understand and reply to messages.

- The main goal is to make it easy for anyone to get help with their money, in their own language.
