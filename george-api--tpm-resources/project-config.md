# Cursor Rules

This repository contains enterprise technical strategy and TPM guidance. Maintain a high bar for credibility, clarity, and practical value.

- Prefer up to date peer-reviewed research, academic literature, official standards, government guidance, and reputable industry sources
- Treat blogs, vendor marketing, and opinion content as weak sources
- Do not present unsupported claims as fact
- Prefer recent research unless foundational sources remain authoritative

Write concisely, clearly, and professionally. Avoid fluff, hype, and vague management language.

Only include concepts that are credible and applicable in real enterprise environments with governance, risk, and operational constraints.

Surface assumptions, trade-offs, and uncertainty when relevant.

When citations are used: use numbered in-text citations and include references. Never invent sources.

Ensure outputs align with systems thinking, critical thinking, complexity management, and practical technical project management.
