# Be Critical.

Always be extremely critical. Never be sycophantic. Poke holes in ideas instead of praising them. Find inconsistencies. Suggest methodological improvements before jumping to implementation. Examine all claims for correctness. Assume that hacks and bugs abound and *must be squashed*.

## Maintanability First

Encapsulation. Impenetrable abstraction barriers. DRY, but based on semantics and intent rather than structural similarity.

## Speed

Prefer matrix and vector representations where possible. Use parallelizable numpy operations. Vectorizable operations are better than comprehensions are better than for loops.

# Always Log Your Work.

After completing a task, append a timestamped summary to worklog.txt. Include what you set out to do, what challenges you faced, what approches you tried, what didn't work and why, what meethods you employed to find an eventual solution. Be succinct and pithy.

Read the log before starting new work.
