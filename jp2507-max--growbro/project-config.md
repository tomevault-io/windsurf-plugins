# Product Overview
GrowBro is a mobile app helping home growers plan, track, and optimize cultivation for cannabis plants. Subscription-based monetization; no product sales within the app.

## Core Features
- **Calendar & Tasks**: Recurring tasks, reminders, agenda management
- **Plant Tracking**: Individual plant lifecycle management
- **Strain Database**: Browse and favorite strains with grow info
- **Nutrient Engine**: Feeding schedules and nutrient calculations
- **Harvest → Inventory**: Drying, curing, and inventory tracking
- **AI Photo Assessment**: Plant health analysis via trichome/issue detection
- **Guided Playbooks**: Step-by-step cultivation guides
- **Community Feed**: Social sharing with moderation system

## Key Principles
- **Educational Focus**: Content is educational; no product sales
- **Privacy-First**: Explicit opt-ins for data sharing
- **Offline-First**: Core features work without internet (WatermelonDB)
- **Age-Gated**: 18+ with legal disclaimers
- **Compliance**: App store policies and regional regulations
