# Ailan

## Project description

I want to build a daily AI health coach powered by Apple Health data (via the apple-health-mcp-server), something genuinely useful, not just a dashboard.
Three layers:
1. Onboarding: the user shares their health context: existing conditions, lifestyle habits (smoking, alcohol, sport frequency), and goals.
2. Daily coaching loop: the coach ingests Apple Watch metrics alongside self-reported inputs (mood, cigarettes, alcohol) and generates personalized, actionable nudges.
3. Pattern recognition: leveraging months of historical data to surface meaningful behavioral trends the user wouldn't notice on their own.
It fits a real product strategy. Alan could offer a discounted Garmin watch in its store as a CAC lever, turning hardware into a coaching entry point. The coach reinforces engagement through a light gamification layer (think "berries" as a dopamine-friendly reward mechanism).
