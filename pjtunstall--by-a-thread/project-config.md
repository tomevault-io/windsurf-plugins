# Comments

- Don't introduce any new comments unless asked to.
- Only use `//` comments, not `///`.
- Comments should adhere to the conventions of standard English punctuation, including a trailing period.
- Comments should be addressed to readers who are not privy to our conversations or previous steps in development, thus avoid comments like `// Infer normal based on which face was hit (original logic).` (We can't expect the reader to be aware of our "original logic".)

## Punctuation in comments and documentation

- Use ' for a single quote or apostrophe, and " for a double quote.
- Use -- for an m-dash.
